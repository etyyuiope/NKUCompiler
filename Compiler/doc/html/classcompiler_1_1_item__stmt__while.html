Generate the IR for while block.

Note
The structure of the while block (also the same for 'for' statement.) is given as follows: .L.scope_WHILE .L.COND CMP JNE DO JEQ END .L.DO DO_SOMETHING BREAK CONITNUE .L.CONITNUE JMP COND .L.END
Insert phi_move before jne END、break;、continue.
